Design Notes

Curated vs Full Catalog

Of the 33,214 total language items, approximately 5,500 items are assigned to levels (the curated curriculum). The remaining ~27,700 items are still accessible via the per-type browser pages (Vocabulary, Grammar, Sentences, etc.) but are not part of the structured level progression. This keeps levels at a manageable density similar to WaniKani's ~143 items/level.

SRS Display

Level page tiles show 4 states: Locked (dashed border), In Lessons (30% opacity), In Reviews (solid color), Burned (gray). This matches the kanji section exactly.
Item detail view shows the full Apprentice 1-4 / Guru 1-2 / Master / Enlightened / Burned progression internally, not on the level tiles.

Unlock Logic (Hybrid)

Level progression gates which items CAN be unlocked (90% Guru+ in level N unlocks level N+1). Per-type lesson caps still control HOW MANY get unlocked at a time, preventing overwhelm.

Item Count Estimates

All item counts marked with ~ are estimates based on migration data analysis. Exact counts will be determined during implementation by querying the actual database. The migration will use existing lesson_group, lesson_order, jlpt_level, and frequency_rank columns to assign items to levels.
